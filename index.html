about

My name is Ryan and I am a high end multi award winning media Generalist and lecturer.

more about me

I have been in the industry for over 16 years. My knowledge extends from storyboarding any project to full completion and getting it fully ready for any media needed. I have managing experience for over 10 years and hands-on experience for over 16 years. My expertise range from conceptual design, digital design, 3D animation, directing, green screens and blue screens, visual effects, CG and live, web design, web build , previz for film and games, creation of game assets, trailers to compositing and completing the project for any media.

I am a person who uses everything and anything to his advantage, whether it be a picture of something I find or something I have to create, or a visual effect to complete a shot. I am always widening my range and live to learn as much as I can. I am a person who does not have to be told to go work, I will always take the initiative and run with it. I have never missed a deadline and I do not intend to ever miss one. I am fully licensed. and have an amazing team I work with on larger projects. I am driven by sheer passion and love of the game and a belief that nothing is impossible!

services

studying and practicing for over 16 years I am a one man band who can work well with teams or alone. I have many skills and am always there when you need me.

3D

From modeling , uv unwrapping, texturing, dynamics, shader build to lighting, rigging, animating and final layer setup to rendering. I know 3D.

compositing

One of my many loves. There is nothing like having all the elements of a shot and putting them together seamlessly.

motion graphics

adding movement and interesting fx. I have done motion graphics for game trailers, corporate videos to TV and Film. This has to be my absolute true love.

give me footage and let me work. Having years of experience from shots for film, full TV adverts to game trailers. I have a ear and an eye to make an edit match perfectly for any occassion. I know editing.
